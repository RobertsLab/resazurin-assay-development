---

### OVERVIEW

Control clams (`C`) compared with selected clams (`S`) at 36<sup>o</sup>C.

Clams were processed in 12-well plates and submerged in `5.0mL` of resazurin working solution.

Plates were measured every 30mins on a Synergy HTX (Agilent) plate reader.

Plate layout was randomized; Steven is currently the only person who knows the treatment assignments.


IMPORTANT NOTES:

- Seemed to be inconsistent heating, with interior wells usually ~1.5<sup>o</sup>C lower than rest of wells.

The following wells have lost volume - possiblly due to clam spitting?

- Plate A, C3
- Plate C, C4
- Plate B, A3
- Plate D, B2

### FILES

Filenames are in the following format: `plate-<A-F>-TN.N.txt`

- `plate-<A-F>`: Plate identifier (randomized treatment layout).
- `*TN.N*`: Time, in `hours.minutes` of exposure at 36<sup>o</sup>C.
