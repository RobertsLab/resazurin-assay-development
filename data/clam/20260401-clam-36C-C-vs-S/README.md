`resazurin-assay-development/data/clam/20260401-clam-36C-C-vs-S`

---

### OVERVIEW

Control clams (`C`) compared with selected clams (`S`) at 36<sup>o</sup>C.

Clams were processed in 12-well plates and submerged in `5.0mL` of resazurin working solution (made 20260330 by SJW; stored @ 4<sup>o</sup>C).

Plates were measured every 30mins on a Synergy HTX (Agilent) plate reader.

Plate layout was randomized; Steven is currently the only person who knows the treatment assignments.

### FILES

Filenames are in the following format: `plate-<G-L>-TN.N.txt`

- `plate-<G-L>`: Plate identifier (randomized treatment layout).
- `*TN.N*`: Time, in `hours.minutes` of exposure at 36<sup>o</sup>C.
