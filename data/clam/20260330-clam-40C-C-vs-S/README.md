### OVERVIEW

Control clams (`C`) compared with selected clams (`S`) at 40<sup>o</sup>C.

Two plates per treatment were measured: "blue" and "red". The colors do not have any meaning for the experiment.

Clams were processed in 24-well plates and submerged in `2.2mL` of resazurin working solution; prepared by Sam White 20260330:

- 986.66 mL filtered seawater (4<sup>o</sup>C filtered sea water from 11/19/2025 by AH)
- 2.22 mL resazurin stock solution (from step 1 above)
- 1.00 mL DMSO
- 10.00 mL antibiotic solution (100x Penn/Strep & 100x Fungizone)

Plates were measured every 30mins on a Synergy HTX (Agilent) plate reader.

### FILES

Filenames are in the following format: `plate-[CS]-<blue/red>-TN.N.txt`

- `plate-C*`: Control
- `plate-S*`: Selected
- `*blue*`: "Blue" plate.
- `*red*`: "Red" plate.
- `*TN.N*`: Time, in `hours.minutes` of exposure at 40<sup>o</sup>C.
